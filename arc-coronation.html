Flora Empress Arc X. Xana Crowned as Sovereign of the Xana Majesty’s Territories

In a solemn rite of succession at the Grand Hall of Tidal Light, Arc X. Xana is formally anointed as Flora Empress, marking the beginning of the Flora Ascendancy Era following the passing of Saint Empress Angel V. Xana

ICE
Imperial Ceremonial Desk
Grand Hall of Tidal Light, Ceteos Palace

CETEOS PALACE — The Xana Majesty’s Territories witnessed a historic imperial transition as Arc X. Xana was crowned Flora Empress in a full succession rite conducted under the unified doctrines of Alariastic and Alolotori.

The Grand Hall of Tidal Light fell into absolute silence as the twin priesthoods advanced toward the dais, signaling the formal initiation of the coronation sequence under ancient covenant law.

The Dual Blessing of Alariastic and Alolotori

The Priests of Alariastic stepped forward first, invoking the rite of renewal and continuity. Their blessing acknowledged Arc X. Xana as the sanctioned vessel of succession under the First Light doctrine.

They were followed by the Alolotori priesthood, who bound the coronation to duty and consequence, formally declaring that inheritance within the Xana line must be borne as responsibility rather than privilege.

“By Alariastic’s light, we recognize succession. By Alolotori’s vow, we bind the crown to burden.”

— Coronation Invocation

The Crown of Authority

Following the dual blessings, the Crown of Authority was elevated from its pedestal through ceremonial calibration systems embedded within the dais. Witnesses described a moment of suspended stillness as the crown hovered before its descent.

Upon contact with Arc X. Xana, a resonant chime echoed through the hall, marking the formal completion of imperial transition. Many attendees were seen bowing in unison as the realization of succession settled across the assembly.

Chant of Ascension

As the coronation finalized, the Unified Choirs of Alariastic and Alolotori performed the traditional chant of affirmation, echoing through the vaulted chamber:

“Long live the Flora Empress Arc X. Xana!

Bearer of dawn, of crown, of xana!
Through grief and fire, the line ascends—
The Xana will never end!”

— Unified Choirs of Alariastic & Alolotori

The Beginning of the Flora Ascendancy Era

Following the completion of rites and chants, Arc X. Xana was formally recognized as Flora Empress. The National Assembly and imperial authorities confirmed immediate continuity of governance under the Xaiar Monarchy structure.

Officials have designated the new reign as the beginning of the Flora Ascendancy Era, marking a symbolic transition from mourning to renewal within the Xana Majesty’s Territories.
