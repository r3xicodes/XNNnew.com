First Official Meeting Between Prince Artur Korol’ and Saint Empress Angel V. Xana Concludes Amid Medical Intervention

Historic imperial audience marked by rare personal blessing as Saint Empress’ condition required early withdrawal from proceedings

XNN
Imperial Correspondence Desk
Xara Palace, Seravell

XARA PALACE, SERAVELL — An official first meeting between Prince Artur Korol’ and Saint Empress Angel V. Xana was held today within the secured medical wing of the Xara Palace, marking a rare diplomatic and personal encounter under strict imperial protocol.

According to palace officials, the audience was conducted in a controlled environment due to the Saint Empress’ ongoing treatment for Stage IV High-Grade Serous Ovarian Carcinoma (HGSC), BRCA-positive / HRD-positive, a condition she has been managing for approximately eight years.

Despite medical limitations, witnesses describe the Empress as “fully aware and composed,” engaging directly with Prince Artur in what sources called a “highly significant exchange of mutual recognition and respect.”

The meeting reportedly carried an unusually personal tone for an imperial-level first contact. Officials confirm that during the exchange, Saint Empress Angel V. Xana offered Prince Artur a formal blessing directed toward “future convergence of events,” a statement interpreted by observers as both symbolic and anticipatory in nature.

"Walk forward without hesitation. What is bound to occur will recognize your steps when the time arrives."

— Saint Empress Angel V. Xana (reported statement)

Palace aides report that Prince Artur remained composed throughout the exchange, though visibly affected by the gravity of the moment. No official transcript of his responses has been released.

The meeting was unexpectedly shortened when medical staff advised immediate withdrawal of the Saint Empress due to worsening fatigue indicators. She was escorted back to her private quarters for continued monitoring and treatment.

Officials emphasize that while her condition remains serious, recent assessments describe her state as “volatile but responsive,” allowing limited ceremonial and diplomatic interaction under supervision.

No further details regarding the private discussion have been disclosed. The imperial court has confirmed that additional briefings may follow once medical stability assessments are complete.
