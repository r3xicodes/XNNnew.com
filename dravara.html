Dravara Conquered and Integrated into Xara

Armed hostile state dismantled in eastern offensive; military assets seized and territory absorbed into mainland under GHXE control

Strategic Plan into Dravara remnants

AX
Arlen D. Xavikos
Xara Palace, Seravell

XARA PALACE, SERAVELL — Imperial command has confirmed that Dravara, formerly a hostile remnant state operating along the eastern frontier, has been fully conquered and formally integrated into Xara, the mainland of the Great Holy Xanarcica Empire.

Unlike Poliskioa and Coronias — which emerged from cartel collapse — Dravara maintained its own armed structure, territorial command, and declared hostility toward GHXE. Intelligence assessments identified it as a destabilizing actor actively consolidating military assets near Xara's border.

The Saint Empress, Saint Empress Angel V. Xana, addressed the Empire during the formal decree:

"Dravara stood in defiance, armed and aligned against us. It chose confrontation. The Crown answered. Dravara is no more — it is now Xara."

— Saint Empress Angel V. Xana

The Campaign of Conquest

Royal Xana Ground Forces initiated a full-spectrum land offensive under severe monsoon conditions. Dravaran forces resisted with entrenched defensive positions, river chokepoints, and fortified jungle compounds.

Lieutenant General Kaevren Solmir, commanding the eastern theater, spoke to XNN:

"This was not stabilization. This was conquest. They fielded artillery, armor, and organized brigades. We dismantled them."

— Lieutenant General Kaevren Solmir

RXAF close air support operated in narrow weather gaps, striking hardened depots and command centers. Artillery units advanced through mud-churned terrain to suppress defensive lines before infantry assaults cleared urban districts.

Within weeks, Dravara's central command collapsed. Surviving officers surrendered unconditionally.

Seizure of Assets

With formal surrender secured, GHXE assumed control of all Dravaran state assets, including:

  • • Military stockpiles and armored vehicles
  • • River transport fleets and port facilities
  • • Industrial production centers
  • • Treasury reserves and state-owned infrastructure

A senior defense official summarized the acquisition:

"They amassed weapons to threaten the frontier. Those same weapons now strengthen it."

— Senior Defense Official

Captured equipment deemed serviceable will be reconditioned under Royal Xana standards. Industrial sites are undergoing assessment for integration into mainland economic grids.

Integration into Xara

Rather than designate Dravara as a colony, the Crown has integrated the territory directly into Xara — the imperial mainland. Under GHXE doctrine, while the geographic term "Xara" refers to the mainland, all unified lands remain Xana under sovereignty of the Crown.

The Saint Empress concluded her proclamation:

"A frontier has become interior. A threat has become strength. Dravara is Xana."

— Saint Empress Angel V. Xana

CONQUEST AND INTEGRATION SUMMARY

  • ▸ Territory Status: Dravara conquered and integrated into mainland Xara—hostile state eliminated
  • ▸ Campaign Type: Full-spectrum offensive under monsoon conditions
  • ▸ Campaign Commander: Lieutenant General Kaevren Solmir (RXGF)
  • ▸ Military Assets: Captured stockpiles, armor, fleets, and industrial infrastructure
  • ▸ Decree Authority: Saint Empress Angel V. Xana

Civil administration, infrastructure integration, and security restructuring are already underway. Former Dravaran territories are being reorganized into mainland provincial districts, eliminating the prior border entirely.

XNN will continue coverage as eastern reconstruction progresses and formerly hostile assets are absorbed into the imperial framework.
