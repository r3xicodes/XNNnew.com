Colonial Leadership Appointed for Poliskioa and Coronias

Saint Empress Angel V. Xana formally issues imperial decrees establishing permanent governance for newly stabilized colonies

Strategic Operation Plan of Op. Twin Tides

TKO
Tavrin K. Olsaro
Xara Palace, Seravell

XARA PALACE, SERAVELL — From the Grand Hall of the Xara Palace in Seravell, capital of the Great Holy Xanarcica Empire, Saint Empress Angel V. Xana formally issued imperial decrees appointing permanent colonial leadership for the newly established Xana Colonies of Poliskioa and Coronias.

The ceremony, attended by senior naval command, Royal Xana Ground Forces representatives, and members of the Imperial Court, marked the final administrative phase following the stabilization campaigns in both territories.

Poliskioa Leadership Decree

By sealed order of the Xana Crown, Lord Veinya L. Kol-Xana has been appointed Head of Colony for Poliskioa.

Standing before assembled officers and court officials, the Saint Empress delivered the decree:

"Poliskioa stands not as conquered soil, but as restored order. Lord Veinya L. Kol-Xana shall govern in my name, and through him, the Crown shall bring structure where corruption once ruled."

— Saint Empress Angel V. Xana

Lord Veinya knelt before the throne dais as the imperial seal was presented. Addressing XNN following the ceremony, he stated:

"I accept this duty not as privilege, but as responsibility. Poliskioa will become a model of disciplined growth and lawful maritime commerce."

— Lord Veinya L. Kol-Xana

Military observers note that Poliskioa's strategic proximity to Trastal and the Apoeln western corridor requires firm administrative oversight paired with sustained infrastructure reconstruction.

Coronias Leadership Decree

In a separate proclamation, the Saint Empress confirmed the appointment of Tidal High Lady Seraphine V. Altairi–Xara as Head of Colony for Coronias.

The appointment was issued upon formal request by Lady Masquerade (Maddie) B. Altairi-Xana, Director of the Maid Corps, whose operational forces played a decisive role in dismantling the island's cartel production networks.

The Saint Empress addressed the reasoning directly:

"Coronias demands vigilance upon the sea and wisdom upon its shores. At the request of Lady Masquerade, and in recognition of loyalty and maritime stewardship, Seraphine V. Altairi–Xara shall guide Coronias under the Crown."

— Saint Empress Angel V. Xana

Tidal High Lady Seraphine spoke briefly following her investiture:

"Coronias will no longer serve shadowed enterprises. Its harbors will be disciplined. Its lands will be cultivated with purpose."

— Tidal High Lady Seraphine V. Altairi–Xara

Lady Masquerade, present in ceremonial uniform, declined extended comment but offered a concise statement to XNN:

"Coronias required someone who understands tides—of water and of power. Seraphine is that leader."

— Lady Masquerade (Maddie) B. Altairi-Xana

Territorial Scope of the New Colonies

Poliskioa's territory spans coastal cities, inland river valleys, and dense jungle belts along the Apoeln western corridor. Coronias, as a portion of a large island, encompasses harbors, cultivated plains, and interior jungles that previously hid illicit production facilities. Both colonies now serve as fortified nodes of imperial governance and commerce.

  • ▸ Issuing Authority: Saint Empress Angel V. Xana
