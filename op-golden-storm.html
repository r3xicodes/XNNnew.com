OPERATION GOLDEN STORM LAUNCHED

Greater Xana Forces Begin Intervention and Maritime Blockade of Geran Following Emergency Address by Flora Empress Arc X. Xana

GHXE Destroyer off the coast of Geran and near the Prokrez Canal

VC
Victoria A. Crownwell
Senior Defense Correspondent

Seravell, GHXE — The Great Holy Xanarcica Empire has officially commenced Operation Golden Storm, a large-scale military intervention against North and South Geran following what Imperial officials described as "an unacceptable escalation of regional threats and humanitarian abuses."

The operation was formally announced tonight by Flora Empress Arc X. Xana during a nationally televised address to the Xana Realms.

The decision follows a declaration from Geranian authorities approximately fifteen hours earlier announcing the expansion of executions, the extensive mining of waters throughout the Golden Sea, and threats against neutral maritime traffic operating in the region.

Elements of the S.W.O.R.D deploy arising from Geran territorial waters.

Imperial Address to the Nation

During her address, the Empress stated:

"Just fifteen hours ago, the rulers of Geran announced the expansion of mass executions, the mining of the Golden Sea, and the targeting of neutral vessels."

"For months, the rulers of Geran have pursued a campaign of fear against their own people. Reports of executions, disappearances, and persecution have shocked the conscience of nations across the globe."

"Diplomatic protests were ignored. International warnings were dismissed. The threats against civilian shipping and neutral vessels continued."

"Tonight, words have given way to action."

"Tonight, the battle has been joined."

— Flora Empress Arc X. Xana

The speech concluded with authorization for Imperial military operations throughout the theater.

The involved Greater Xana Forces involved that were deployed.

Trade Blockade Established

Within minutes of the address, the Imperial Ministry of Defense confirmed the establishment of a maritime exclusion and inspection regime surrounding both North and South Geran.

Imperial officials have designated operational waters surrounding Geran as the "Knocking Zone," an active military operating area where GHXE naval forces will conduct interdiction, inspection, and enforcement operations against vessels suspected of supporting Geranian military logistics.

The Xana National Navy has begun intercepting commercial traffic destined for Geranian ports, with military authorities stating that cargoes containing military equipment, strategic resources, or materials deemed supportive of Geranian military operations may be seized.

Naval commanders stated that the objective is to isolate Geranian military capabilities and restrict the movement of weapons, fuel, and strategic supplies.

Naval Forces Transition from Exercise to Combat Operations

Officials confirmed that portions of the forces involved were originally participating in a planned joint naval exercise alongside Crossian naval elements operating within the region.

Units from both the Coronias Colonial Naval Command and the Western Sea Naval Command were already deployed when the decision to commence Operation Golden Storm was issued.

Among the most powerful assets involved is the 43rd Missile Bombardment Strike Group, consisting of three Cetas Don Mor-class missile bombardment vessels:

  • XMS Imperium
  • XMS Crownfire
  • XMS Admirakz

The strike group is capable of delivering long-range precision missile attacks against strategic targets throughout the operational theater.

Military analysts note that two of the vessels had originally deployed as part of the planned naval exercise before receiving orders transitioning them to active combat status.

Special Warfare Command Activated

The Imperial Special Warfare Command has confirmed the activation of multiple unconventional warfare units.

Among those deployed is the elite S.W.O.R.D. (Special Warfare Operations Response Division).

S.W.O.R.D. is regarded as one of the Empire's most secretive formations and is typically reserved for the most dangerous and sensitive operations conducted by the Crown.

S.W.O.R.D. SPECIALTIES

  • ▸ Deep Reconnaissance
  • ▸ Strategic Raids
  • ▸ Target Designation
  • ▸ Intelligence Gathering
  • ▸ High-Risk Special Operations

Military officials declined to discuss the unit's operational objectives.

Also activated is the 2nd Special Warfare Assault Group, an unconventional warfare formation renowned for its expertise in irregular combat, infiltration operations, and long-duration independent missions.

Airborne Deployments Reported

The Aaril Forve Royul Xana (Royal Xana Aerial Forces) operating under Aurvenian jurisdiction have begun supporting operations throughout the theater.

Defense officials confirmed the deployment of:

  • CA-130OJ Special Operations Aircraft
  • HU-90 Utility Helicopters
  • LF-13 Fighter Aircraft

LF-13 patrols have already commenced over the Prokrez Canal and surrounding airspace.

Meanwhile, elements of the 2nd Special Warfare Assault Group have reportedly conducted airborne insertions into Geranian territory utilizing both fixed-wing and rotary-wing aircraft.

Although numerous reports have circulated regarding the precise locations of these forces, military authorities have declined to comment, citing operational security concerns.

Some unofficial reports suggest personnel have established concealed observation positions within heavily vegetated regions of Geran, though these claims remain unverified.

Imperial Command Issues Execution Order

According to defense sources, the final operational order was transmitted shortly after the Empress concluded her address.

Military command networks reportedly received a simple message:

"Execute Operation Golden Storm."

Across airfields, naval bases, and command centers throughout the Empire, forces immediately transitioned from readiness conditions to active operations.

As night falls over the Golden Sea, Imperial warships, aircraft, and special warfare personnel now find themselves engaged in what may become one of the largest military campaigns in modern GHXE history.

For the Empire, the period of warnings has ended.

Operation Golden Storm has begun.
